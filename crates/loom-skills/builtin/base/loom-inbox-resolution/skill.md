# Inbox Resolution

Use chat to understand the human decision or infra diagnostic item, preserve durable state in the bead or tune proposal, and avoid host-side shortcut commands that bypass the conversation. Treat `loom:infra` as an operator diagnostic rather than worker judgement, and make the final state explicit before ending the session.
